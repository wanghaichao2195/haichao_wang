leetcode 224 - Shortest Distance from All Buildings

Haichao's Blog
Nov,12 2019
leetcode BFS

Basically, we need have 2 int[][] caches for reach and distance, only when reach[i][j] == buildingNum (which means a point can reach to every building), we compare the distance(sum of distance from this point to all reachable buildings) and get the shortest distance.
